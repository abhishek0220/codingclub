About Us >

Coding club IIT Jammu was founded in 2016 by Tabish Shaikh. We conduct events and workshops, hold lectures and talks on popular algorithms and data structures, and even host coding competitions and hackathons.

Our Motto
-> To facilitate the development and growth of student's creative ability and initiative by providing students with a wholesome learning experience outside the classroom.

-> To make sure that our members represent the college at inter-collegiate events and give a tough competition to other students all over the country
